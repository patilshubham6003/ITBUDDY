Please refer to the above.
2.     Vide your above referred e-mail, you have raised following objection against the proposed allotment of type-IV(S) quarters for cut off period ending on 31.07.2022:
i)    The seniority of Shri Sourav Jain, DCIT (Civil Code-17010) over you is incorrect according to the General Pool Residential Allocation (GPRA-Rules) dated 19.06.2017.
ii)    Your request made by email dated-30.03.2022 for allotment of residence being extreme hardship faced by you due to infant child of 1 year.
iii)    Your preference for allotment of type-IV quarter was wrongly mentioned 1st in the proposed allotment as your first choice is for allotment of type- IV(S) quarter.
3.    In this regard I am directed to convey to your that the seniority cum waiting list for type-IV(S) quarters alongwith list of vacant Quarter for cut off period ending on 31.07.2022 were published vide notice No.Pr.CC.MUM/Admin/Accom/Type-IV Spl./2022-23 dated-03.08.2022, seeking objection with regard to seniority, if any by 10.08.2022. But no objection was filed for the same. As no objection was filed by any officer against the seniority cum waiting list published vide notice dated- 03.08.2022, the same was finalized. Accordingly, proposed allotment was finalized based on the preferences and seniority. Further, proposed allotment were published vide notice dated-11.08.2022, seeking objection if any with regard to preferences for allotment.
4.     As no objection was filed by you with regard to seniority cum waiting list published vide notice dated-03.08.2022 therefore, the objection raised by you at the time of proposed allotment of type-IV(S) quarters is not found justifiable.
5.     With regard to your objection that preference for allotment of type-IV quarter was wrongly mentioned 1st in the proposed allotment as your first choice is for allotment of type-IV(S) quarter. In this regard, I am directed to state that allotment of type-IV and type-IV(S) quarter are mutually exclusive. Therefore, preferences for each type of allotment are asked in separate mail. In the preference given by you for allotment of type-IV quarter, first choice is Quarter No.239, Bandra. Hence, being senior in the seniority cum waiting list for allotment of type-IV quarters, the same is allotted to you.
6.     In view of the above, your objections are disposed off. However, in next cycle of allotment you may submit your preference for allotment and your application will be considered as per your contention and prevailing rules.



(AKHIL GOAL)
Dy.Commissioner of Income-tax(HQ)
Administration, Mumbai
