ALLOTMENT OF GOVERNMENT RESIDENTIAL ACCOMMODATION
      FROM INCOME-TAX POOL AT MUMBAI-reg.

..............


      The marginally noted residence Quarter No. C-22 (Type-IV Special), Income Tax Colony, Carmichael Road, Cumballa Hill, Off Peddar Road, Mumbai-400026, is hereby allotted to Smt. Jancey Elizabeth Rani, 3CIT (Civil Code-12074), Mumbal in accordance with and subject to the provisions of the CBDT Residential in the I.T Pool.

     2      This allotee is hereby requested to convey his/her acceptance within eight dayfrom the date of receipt of this letter.The acceptance should be in the enclosed from and shoyld be forwarded to the undersigned by name

      3       This allotment is subject to the terms and conditions set out herein under:-

      3.1       If the official fails to accept the allotment of resdance within eight days or fails to take possession of the residence after acceptance within five working days form the date of receipt of this letter of allotment,he/she shall not be eligible for another allotment for a period of 3 months from the date of this allotment letter. Further, he/she will be charged license fee which he/she would have to pay under F.R.45A in respect of the residance already in occupation,whenever is higher, from the date of issue of this letter

      3.2       If the official is temporary Govt.servant , he/she is required to purnish a Surety bond on prescribed from (Schedule'B') from a permanent Central Govt.Servant alongwith the acceptance as per the provisions contained in S.R.317-p-17.

      3.3     If the allotment is accepted, the official should obtain an occupation slip from his office and take possession of the allotted residence from the concerned CPWD office (in respect of Quarters at Antop Hill), Caretaker (in respect of quarters at Bhandup, Goregaon Hostel, Goregaon Type-III, Powal, Belapur and Jogeshwar) and this office (in respect of quarters at Peddar Road, Goregaon Type IV, Cassi Mitha and Bandra) within eight days from the date of receipt of this letter. In case of failure to take possession within the time specified above, the allotment will be treated as cancelled and the quarter will be allotted to the next eligible officer.

      3.4     The allottee have to intimate this office in writing that he has taken physical possession of allotted quarter within 8 days after filing acceptance of allotted quarter. If allottee falls to take physical possession within 8 days, the allotment will be treated as cancelled unless the allottee provides reason in writing to this office. The extension for physical possession will be provided after perusal of reasons provided in writing. The decision of extension will solely be of competent authority. If the competent authority is not satisfied with the reasons provided, the allotment will be cancelled and this decision of competent authority will be final.

      3.5     The allottee will be charged license fee under F.R.45A and the liability thereof will commence from the 8th day from the receipt of this letter or from the date of taking over possession, whichever is earlier. In addition, water charges and share of taxes should be paid by him/her as in force.

      3.6     In case of change or allotment of another type of quarter, shifting should be done within thirty days from the date of receipt of this letter. If both the residences are retained for more than eight days, license fee at the market rate for the previous residence will be charged in addition to the normal license fee of the new residence. Without prejudice to this, this allotment would be liable to be cancelled.

      3.7     The allottee shall not make any additions and/or alterations to the premises allotted to him/her.

      3.8     The allottee will have to get meter and electric connection in the said residence by paying Security Deposit and connection fee to the BEST Undertaking/BSES/MSEB after accepting the allotment of the residence. He/She will have to obtain application form and test certificate duly signed by the concerned Jr/Asst/Executive Engineer (Elec), CPWD, Mumbal, but his/her occupation of the said residence cannot be made contingent upon provisions of such facility. Similarly, whitewashing or removal of defects, if any, would be carried out by the CPWD authorities according to the prevalent rules and hence, occupation of the said residence cannot be made contingent upon provision of such facility And further, this office would not entertain any correspondance in this connection. Therefore, the allottee may be advised to take over possession of the residence with the stipulated time and get any such work done thereafter

      3.9      The electricity connection and all pending charges should be settled by the allottee with the electricity supply authorities concerned directly at the time of taking physical possession. This office will not entertain any correspondence regarding payment of electric charges whatsoever.

3.10     This allotmentis made to him/her subject to the condition that he/she is not eligible for allotment of Govt. Residence in General Pool and hence, he/she is not considered for allotment in that pool and is not occupying any accommodation from the general pool

      3.11      As per present instruction of the Board, officers entitled for Type-VI quarters, but who have been allotted accommodation in the departmental pool must apply for the general pool accommodation and in the event of allotment of accommodation by the Estate Manager, Mumbe, they should shit to that accommodation Failure to comply with entail recovery of rent at market rate, in force from them

      3.12      This alotment is made subject to the condition that the spouse of the allottee is not occupying accommodation from General Pool or any other Pool. This certificate may please be fumished at the time of submitting the Actiptance Letter

      3.13      A declaration to the effect that the officer concerned does not own a house etc. or other wise should be furnish as provided in the Acceptance Letter

      3.14      This Govt. Accommodation has been allotted for the bonafide use of the allotted and his/ her family. Subletter or sharing of the premises with out prior approval of this office is not permissible. If an allottee unauthosedly sublets or shares the residence, without prejudice to any other action (including action to cancel the alllotment) that may be taken against him/her, he/she will be charged enhanced rent upto four times the Standard Rent plus service charges per month. Where after the allotment has been cancelled or is deemed to be cancelled under any provision of the Revenue Allotment Rules, the residence remains or has remained in occupation of the allotteeor any person claiming through him, the allottee shall be liable to pay market rent in force

      3.15      This alotment is made subject to the condition that the allottee is continuously employed and remains employed in the Income Tax Department, at Mumbai

      3.16      In case the offical is not willing to accept the house offered above and wishes to consider his request in some other area, he can technically accept the allotment by filling up the technical acceptance form and the change form available from the office of the DCIT(HQ) Admn., mumbai. In case of technical acceptance only one month additional licence fee will be charged and HRA shall continue to be drawn if the allottee is not in physical occupation of the government accommodation

      3.17      While surrendering the accommodation, the allotee is required to give 10 days in writing before vacating the said premises to this office and if he/she fails to do so, he/she will be responsible for payment of rent for that period or number of days by which notice given by him/her fails short of 10 days from the date of vacation

      3.18      The premises includes fixtures and fittings shall be handed over by allotee in good condition at the time of vacation falling which the cost of damages will be recovered from him/her. The inventory of cvil/electric and other fittings fatures taken over should be sent along with letter of surrender. Keys of the said premises should be handed over to the persons as mentioned in para 5 above at the time of surrounding the same.

      3.19      The Bill of Landline Telephone Connection, Internet connection and Electricity connection should be cleared by the allottee at the time of vacating the allotted accommodation. This office will not entertain any correspondence regarding payment of telephone, internet or electricity connection whatsoever. At the time of surrender, copies of last telephone bill, internet bill and electricity bill, duly paid, should be attached with the surrender letter. The surrender of accommodation will be accepted only if the above documents are provided. Until and unlest, this office issue a surrender certificate, the allotment will not be declared surrendered by the allottee.

      3.20      The allotte should maintain proper decorum in the society and should not be involved in any activity which falls under public nuisance. If Residents Welfare Association of the Residential Complex complains against the alotter in relation to the activities which comes under the ambit of public nuisance then the said allotee may be requested to vacate the quarters after giving due opportunity.
