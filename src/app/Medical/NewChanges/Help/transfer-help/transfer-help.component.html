Claim Information Form
Please provide accurate and complete details for processing your claim. This information is crucial for ensuring timely and accurate reimbursement. Follow these steps to fill out the claim information:
1. Relationship with Applicant : Indicate your relationship with the applicant (patient), such as self, spouse, child, etc.
2. Patient Name : Enter the full name of the patient (applicant).
3. Patient CGHS Beneficiary Number :
      - Enter the CGHS beneficiary number assigned to the patient.
4. Nature Of Treatment/Test :
      - Specify the nature of the treatment or test undergone by the patient (e.g., "CANCER").
5. Treatment Start Date :
      - Enter the date when the treatment or test started
6. Treatment End Date :
      - Enter the date when the treatment or test ended.
7. Bill Filling Date :
      - Enter the date when the bill for the treatment or test was filled in the office.
8. Is Advance Taken? :
      - Indicate whether an advance payment was taken for the treatment (Yes/No).
9. Hospital Type :
      - Choose the appropriate hospital type from the options provided. (Empanelled /Non empanelled)
10. Hospital Name :
      - Enter the name of the hospital where the treatment or test was conducted.
11. Hospital Address :
      - Enter the complete address of the hospital.
12. Accreditations :
      - Indicate if the hospital is non-empanelled, and provide accreditation details if applicable.
13. Bank Details :
      -Enter bank details
14. Ward Entitlement :
      - Select the type of ward entitlement (Pvt., Semi Pvt., General) for the treatment.
15. Treatment Type :
      - Specify whether the treatment is for OPD (Outpatient Department) or indoor treatment.
16. OPD Treatment / Test Entitled :
      - Indicate the entitled OPD treatment or test.
17. Indoor Treatment :
      - Provide details of indoor treatment, if applicable.
