LTC

LTC claims refer to the process by which eligible employees submit requests for reimbursement of travel expenses incurred during their approved leave period. LTC is a benefit provided by some employers, particularly in government organizations, to help employees cover the cost of traveling.

TOUR

Tour expenses related to official business travel are typically reimbursed by the employer and are not treated as taxable income for the employee. These reimbursements are considered as part of the employment agreement and are not subject to income tax.

TRANSFER

Employee transfer allowances in the context of income tax generally refer to the financial support provided by an employer to an employee who is required to relocate due to a job transfer. These allowances can help the employee cover the costs associated with moving to a new location.
